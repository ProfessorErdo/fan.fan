Investment Framework

Four Quadrants Framework

Classify stocks by quality (ROE) and price (PE ratio) to find investment opportunities

Q4: Target Zone

High ROE + Low PE. High-quality companies at attractive valuations. Primary investment candidates.

Q1: Premium Quality

High ROE + High PE. Excellent companies but expensive. Wait for price correction.

Q2: Avoid

Low ROE + High PE. Poor quality at premium prices. The worst quadrant.

Q3: Value Trap?

Low ROE + Low PE. Cheap but questionable quality. Investigate further.
